About

The name Lilas has been created from the beautiful French word meaning flower. Just as flower blossoms, your experience here will be blossoming.

The Hostel is located in Grand Baie, a beautiful tourist village in Mauritius. It affords its residents an opportunity to meet with great and beautiful students from the world over.

The body of students who reside in this hostel are drawn from great universities spread across the beautiful island of Mauritius. At Lilas, you feel welcome and at home.

Local Attractions

Tamarind Falls

The Tamarind Falls (also known as Tamarin Falls or 7 cascades) are a beautiful attraction of the southwest of Mauritius. Popular for its beautiful surroundings and unspoilt nature, the Tamarind fall is an astonishing albeit hard-to-reach area.

Mont Choisy Beach

Mon Choisy Beach is located near the village of Grand Baie, in the northwest part of Mauritius. This is one of the many beaches of Mauritius situated in the Pamplemousses district.It is a few km away from the hostel.

Botanical Garden

The Botanic Garden, formally known as Sir Seewoosagur Botanic Garden, is one of the most visited attractions in Mauritius.The garden is located in the proximity of Port-Louis.It may take you more than a week to cover the whole garden
